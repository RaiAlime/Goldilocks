About Goldilocks Philippines

Goldilocks Bakeshop, Inc. is a leading Filipino bakery and food service company that has become a staple in Filipino celebrations and everyday life.

Our Story & Heritage

Founded in 1966 in Makati, Metro Manila, Goldilocks has grown into one of the largest Filipino-owned bakeshop chains known for quality baked goods and food offerings.
